How does it work?

Every Skin.Club user can take part in a limited-time event. As part of the event, you can participate in leaderboards and giveaways. Earn and spend event points in the marketplace. Read more about event updates in the article: Learn more

  1. What is The Dragon Awaits your victory?

    The Dragon Awaits your victory on SC is a time-limited event that gives you a unique opportunity to get tons of awesome free skins!

  2. How can I get Dragon Points?

    You can get Dragon Points for buying cases, upgrading skins and battles.

  3. How do event leaderboards work?

    Play on SC and earn Dragon Points. The more Dragon Points you earn in a week, the better your reward. To get the reward you need to be in the top players on the active leaderboard.

  4. What is a giveaway ticket?

    It's a paid method for taking part in one of 5 of our draws. You can buy giveaway tickets from the marketplace. You can spend your points until the end of the giveaway timer.

  5. What is a marketplace?

    This is the place where you can exchange all your earned Dragon Points for skins, cases, or giveaway tickets. Event points can be accumulated and will be transferred to the next event so that you can buy a cooler prize. The marketplace`s assortment is updated once a week, so stay tuned.

  6. How can I enter a giveaway?

    To be able to enter a giveaway, you need to spend a specific amount of event points in the marketplace. Different giveaways require different amount of event points. To enter a giveaway, you have to buy at least one giveaway ticket with Dragon Points and put it in the giveaway. For users with SC level over 50, a premium giveaway is always available and they don't need to spend Dragon Points to unlock it.
